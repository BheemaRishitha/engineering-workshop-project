STRIKING TOOLS

1)Mallet: A heavy-headed tool used for striking chisels or driving nails, often made of rubber or wood for softer materials.

2)Claw Hammer: A versatile hammer with a forked claw on the back for pulling nails

3)Screwdriver: A tool with a metal shaft and a specialized tip that fits the head of a screw for turning it.

4)Wood Rasp File: A coarse file with a rough surface used for shaping and smoothing wood.
