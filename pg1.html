MARKING AND MEASURING TOOLS

These tools are essential for accurate measuring and marking in various projects.

1) Rules & Straight Edge

Flat tools used for drawing straight lines and checking flatness. A ruler is a marked rule, while a straight edge is unmarked.

2) Gauges

Adjustable tools used for marking parallel lines at specific distances from a reference edge.

3) Try Square

L-shaped tool with a perfectly square blade used for checking right angles (90 degrees) and drawing perpendicular lines.
