MARKING AND MEASURING TOOLS

1)Rules & Straight Edge: Flat tools for drawing straight lines and checking flatness. A ruler is a marked rule, while a straight edge is unmarked.

2)Gauges: Adjustable tools used for marking parallel lines at specific distances from a reference edge.

3)Try Square: L-shaped tool with a perfectly square blade used for checking right angles (90 degrees) and drawing perpendicular lines.
