PLANNING TOOLS

These are some essential tools for woodworking planning:

Jack Plane

The workhorse of hand planes. Used for general smoothing and flattening of rough surfaces. Longer sole than smoothing planes for better control.

Smoothing Plane

The finishing touch! Creates a smooth, polished surface after the jack plane has done its job. Shorter sole for precise control.

Rebate Plane

Cuts a groove or channel along the edge of a board. Perfect for creating dados and other joinery features. Adjustable blade depth for precise control.

Plough Plane

Grooves for life! Cuts wide grooves or channels in wood, often used for installing drawer slides or decorative moldings. Features a fence for straight cuts and adjustable blades for different groove widths.
