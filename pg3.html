PLANNING TOOLS

1)Jack Plane: The workhorse of hand planes. Used for general smoothing and flattening of rough surfaces. Longer sole than smoothing planes for better control.

2)Smoothing Plane: The finishing touch! Creates a smooth, polished surface after the jack plane has done its job. Shorter sole for precise control.

3)Rebate Plane: Cuts a groove or channel along the edge of a board. Perfect for creating dados and other joinery features. Adjustable blade depth for precise control.

4)Plough Plane: Grooves for life! Cuts wide grooves or channels in wood, often used for installing drawer slides or decorative moldings. Features a fence for straight cuts and adjustable blades for different groove widths.
