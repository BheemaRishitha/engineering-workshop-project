CUTTING TOOLS

There are various cutting tools used for woodworking, each with its specific purpose:

1) **Cross-cut saw:** Designed for clean cuts across the wood grain, with teeth angled like tiny chisels.

2) **Rip saw:** Makes smooth cuts along the wood grain, featuring teeth with a straight, square edge for efficient splitting.

3) **Tenon saw:** Thin blade backed with a metal spine for stability, perfect for precise cuts in joinery like tenons.

4) **Chisels:** Hand tools with sharpened blades used for controlled carving, chopping, and cleaning cuts in wood.
